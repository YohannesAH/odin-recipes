Steps

  1. Boil Fresh Pasta Dough
  2. Weigh the Flour. Set your scale to grams
  3. Beat the Eggs. Crack two eggs into a medium bowl
  4. Form a Well
  5. Add Eggs and Fold
  6. Break Up Large Clumps
  7. Make a Ball of Dough
  8. Knead and Rest the Dough
